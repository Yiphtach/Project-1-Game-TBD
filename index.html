How to Play

Welcome to Tamagotchi! Keep your virtual pet happy by managing its needs:

  • Play: Reduces boredom.
  • Feed: Reduces hunger.
  • Sleep: Reduces sleepiness.

Keep all needs below 10 to prevent your Tamagotchi from becoming too sad!
